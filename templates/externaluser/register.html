Welcome to Freva – Free Evaluation System Framework!

Feel free to register for our BETA phase of the Shell and Web system.
Browse, click around and explore our command line system. See what Freva is capable of as projects, working groups or institutes evaluation infrastructure in the earth system science community. This instance Freva is for show cases and no platform which guarantees you work environment. We are happy to show you what Freva can do for you, and your institute. Freva takes care that you don’t get lost in your climate science adventure by advancing transparency and reproducibility. With an eye on usability and flexibility, Freva gives you the full control of your data and software. By registering you agree to the terms and condtions.

Terms and Conditions

Freva is software developed within the MiKlip INTEGRATION project by the Institute for Meteorology at the Freie Universität Berlin Germany. The projects funded by the Minestry of Teaching and Research (BMBF) in Germany.

By registering with your real name, email address, and institution you allow the hosts of Freva to save these informations for the terms of system related actions, like account set up, quota problems, Guidance, etc.
The hosts will NOT give your data to any third party related group.
You are NOT allowed to download any data from the data pool, or software code plugged-in - besides your own.
Freva is NOT a data portal for downloading reanalysis, observations, CMIP, or similar. Analyzing these data sets and downloading the results is of course fine.

The Freva hosts are allowed to delete, lock, freeze and change the system, the terms and conditions and its user account at any time. It is a developmental system, therefore it gaves no guarantee for a stable usage and longterm archive for results. This system is a showcase system that should show you what Freva is capable of.
